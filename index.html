What is Moodasort?

Moodasort is able to identify patterns and trends that correspond with specific moods, and can then suggest tracks that are most likely to resonate with the user's emotional state. Overall, Moodasort represents a major breakthrough in the field of music curation and discovery, and is sure to revolutionize the way that people interact with their music libraries.
